Detailed Description

2D structured mesh in 3D.

Grid is 2D but it is embedded in 3D space. Type used for co-ordinates is vec3.

Generates coordinates of the structured 2D mesh embedded in 3D for a parametric surface according to functions specifying the x, y, and z coordinates in terms of three scalar functions of 2 variables. Coordinates are obtained by transforming individual coordinates of a regular equidistant grid lying on XY plane from the reference system.
