Detailed Description

Alternative parameterization of an origin-centered hyperboloid surface (x^2/a^2+y^2/b^2-z^2/c^2=1) in form of 3D vector function of 2 variables.

One set of parameter curves are straight lines running in counter clockwise direction when watched from above.

Parameterization is similar to HyperboloidParametricPlus, with difference in signs. $A Igor Oct09;
